ButterChicken-Recipe

Description:

Butter Chicken also known as Chicken Makhani is a classic indian dish
that's made by simmmering marinated & grilled chicken in a spicy,
aromatic, buttery and creamy tomato gravy.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless Chicken.
  • Butter,Cream.
  • Garlic,Onion.
  • Tomato,Chili pepper.
  • Spices.

Steps Of Prepration:

  1. Marinade 500 grams of boneless Chicken with lemon juice, salt,curd/greek yogurt,vegetable oil and different spices.
  2. Prepare the sauce/gravy by blending 600 grams of diced tomatoes and also prepare cashew cream by blending cashews.
  3. Now take a pan and heat 2 tablespoon of butter then add cinnamon,cloves and cardamons.
  4. Once they begin to sizzle, add ginger garlic paste,green chilies and fry ona medium heat for 1 to 2 minutes till the raw smell goes away.
  5. Now add the blended tomatoes to the pan and boil for 15 mins then add cashew cream and stir the cashew cream well.
  6. Add 1½ of water to pan and simmer for 10 minutes with partially covered till the sauce thickens and traces of fat are visible.
  7. Add one tablespoon to a pan and fry the marinated chicken on the medium heat till all the moisture evaporates.
  8. Now add the fried chicken to the early prepared gravy and add ½ of hot water,1 teaspoon of sugar and add #189 of kasuri methi.
  9. Add ¼ to ½ cup of heavy cream,stir and Now Your Butter Chicken is ready.
