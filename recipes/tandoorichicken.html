TandooriChicken-Recipe

Description:

Tandoori Chicken, also known as murgh tandoori, is a popular North Indian appetizer
where bone-in chicken is marinated with lime juice, yogurt, and aromatic spices and
then roasted or grilled until perfection. It is best served with green chutney, sliced onions, and lime wedges.

Ingredients:

  • ½ kg of chicken.
  • ½ cup greek yogurt.
  • Ginger garlic paste, garam masala.
  • Red chilli powder, crushed black pepper.
  • Turmeric, kasuri methi.
  • Lemon juice and 1½ tablespoon of oil

Steps Of Prepration:

  1. Heat the oil in a small pan over medium heat, then cook the spices, stirring often, until fragrant (approximately 2-3 minutes). Let cool completely.
  2. Whisk spices into yogurt, add lemon juice, garlic, salt, ginger
  3. Cut deep slashes into chicken, coat with marinade and Prepare your grill so that one side is quite hot over direct heat, the other side cooler, not over direct heat.
  4. Shake off excess marinade, place chicken on hot side of grill
  5. Move chicken to the cool side of the grill. Cover and cook for at least 20 minutes, up to 40 minutes (or longer) depending on the size of the chicken and the temperature of the grill.
  6. The chicken is done when its juices run clear.
